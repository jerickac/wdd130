Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide an online resource to schedule trips and get information about them

Audience

Q.: Who are the target customers? Describe their life (or business) situation (age, financial situation, interests, and so on)
A.: The target customers are businesspeople, around their 40s, being in a very good financial position, let's say class A, they like the nature and outdoor group activities.
Q.: What do they want?
A.: They want to experience more intense type of sports.
Q.: What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website?
A.: By accessing the website they can schedule their trips on any level they want, know informations about the places where they can go, about all the equipments and thus guarantee their safety.
