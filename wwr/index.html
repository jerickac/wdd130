Explore Our Exciting Trips!

At JR Rafting, our purpose is to provide adrenaline-pumping experiences that connect people with the thrill of white water rafting. We're passionate about creating unforgettable memories in nature's playground, fostering a sense of adventure, camaraderie, and appreciation for the great outdoors. Our mission is to deliver safe, exhilarating rafting trips led by expert guides, ensuring every participant feels empowered to conquer the rapids and immerse themselves in the beauty of our planet's most spectacular rivers

  • Guided Rafting Tours: Embark on guided rafting tours led by experienced and certified guides. Explore a variety of rivers with different difficulty levels, catering to everyone from beginners to seasoned adventurers.
  • Customized Adventures: Tailor your rafting experience with customizable trip options. Choose from half-day, full-day, or multi-day excursions, and select the level of intensity and scenery that suits your preferences.
  • Safety First: Rest assured with our commitment to safety. Our guides are trained in first aid, river rescue techniques, and are equipped with top-of-the-line safety gear to ensure a secure and enjoyable journey for all participants.
  • Equipment Rental: Don't have your own gear? No problem! Rent high-quality rafting equipment, including rafts, paddles, helmets, and personal flotation devices, for your adventure.
  • Family-Friendly Trips: Bring the whole family along for a memorable outdoor adventure. We offer family-friendly rafting trips suitable for all ages, complete with calm stretches for relaxing floats and exciting rapids for a bit of thrill.
  • Group Packages: Plan an unforgettable group outing with our group packages. Perfect for corporate team-building events, school trips, bachelor/bachelorette parties, and more. Enjoy discounted rates and personalized itineraries for your group's needs.
  • Scenic River Tours: Immerse yourself in the beauty of nature with our scenic river tours. Glide through picturesque landscapes, spotting wildlife and marveling at stunning natural wonders along the way.
  • Adventure Photography: Capture every exhilarating moment with our adventure photography services. Professional photographers will document your rafting experience, providing you with lasting memories to cherish for years to come.
